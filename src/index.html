About Us

Our Story

Since 2016, Jack's Bargain Barn has been serving the Hinton community with incredible deals on everyday essentials. When Jack Crook and his wife Tina were presented with a life-changing decision - pursuing nursing school or opening a retail store - they chose to embark on this entrepreneurial journey together.

Strategically located next to Save-A-Lot on Main Street, Jack waited patiently for this prime spot to become available. His belief in the importance of location has proven successful, as the store has been thriving since its opening. Our commitment to customer satisfaction remains unchanged since day one - ensuring every customer leaves happy with quality products at unbeatable prices.
